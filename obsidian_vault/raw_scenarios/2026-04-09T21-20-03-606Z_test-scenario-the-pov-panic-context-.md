Moe is at the Cash Register but is visibly "buffering" because the line is getting long. Dianara is at the Traditional Bar in the back, laughing at a customer who just asked if the "matcha is gluten-free." Adam is at the Sinks, deep in a logic loop about a database migration, staring at a dirty pitcher.

The Action:
Lavanya enters the floor, not with a milk jug, but with her phone on a gimbal. She sees the chaos and decides it's the perfect time for a "POV: You’re a Barista at Qahwtea" Reel.

She walks to the back and tells Adam to "stay in that 404-glitch pose" because it’s "relatable content." Then, she tries to force a station-swap: she wants Moe to move to the Juice Bar so she can film him looking "peaceful" while Dianara takes over the Register to show off her "Lobby Energy."

The Friction:

Moe just wants to finish the shift without being on the main feed.

Heba is trying to keep the drinks moving while Lavanya keeps asking her to "slow down the pour for the aesthetic."

Adam finally "reboots" and realizes he’s been staring at the same spoon for three minutes while a sink is overflowing.
