# Adam

**Role:** Protagonist / The Visionary

## Profile

Adam is the kind of person who debugs distributed systems in his head while pulling espresso shots. By day (and most nights), he's deep in the architecture of a Smart Warehouse B2B platform — routing algorithms, inventory state machines, edge-case logic that would put most engineers to sleep. But here he is, working the morning shift at Qahwtea, because the chaos of a rush hour somehow helps him think.

## Core Traits

- **Technically brilliant, socially absorbed.** He's not oblivious — he notices everything — he just processes it on a delay, like a thread waiting on a mutex.
- **The Visionary.** Sees systems where others see noise. A backed-up coffee queue is a throughput problem. A bad morning is a UX failure.
- **Ritual-driven.** The coffee-making process is sacred. Grind size, water temp, extraction time — this is not a job, it's a proof of concept.
- **Slow to speak, deliberate when he does.** When Adam says something, people tend to listen, even if they're not sure why.

## Relationship to Setting

The cafe is his liminal space — between builds, between versions of himself. He didn't plan to stay this long.
